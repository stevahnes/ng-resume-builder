What is NgResume?

NgResume is an automatic and open-source resume generator. It was born out of the frustration from having to meddle with document editors whenever it's time to update our resume. Acknowledging that there are numerous similar resume generators out there, we designed NgResume towards the formats typically accepted in Singapore. We also hope to make it open-source, so that anyone with some proficiency in programming can create a pull request and upload their own templates. The idea is for NgResume to eventually be a community-driven resume generator app!
